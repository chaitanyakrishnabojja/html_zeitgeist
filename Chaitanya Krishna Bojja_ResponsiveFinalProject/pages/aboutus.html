About

Zietgeist-2k18 is a 2-day national level technical symposium organised by ECE department of the prestigious JNTU Kakinada University.This year, we are organising the sixth edition of Zietgeist and the past editions are highly successful.We assure a worthy participation and a wonderful stay here at Kakinada.

ZIETGEIST provides a platform to the youth to showcase their talents and skill sets in fierce competitions, display cutting edge technology and innovation from all over the state. This year Zietgeist plans to be even bigger and better than before. One can definitely expect an altogether new spin to the things already done and something far more exciting and amazing like never seen before.
